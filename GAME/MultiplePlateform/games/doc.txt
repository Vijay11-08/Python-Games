# 🎮 Multi-Game Platform - Documentation 🚀🎲  

## 📄 Introduction 🕹️✨  
Welcome to the **Multi-Game Platform**! 🎉 This project is a **collection of 10 exciting and interactive games**, designed to **entertain, challenge, and engage players** of all ages. 🧠💡 Whether you love **puzzles, logic games, reflex challenges, or strategy-based gameplay**, this platform has something for you! 🤩  

The platform provides a **visually stunning interface** with a **smooth navigation system**, allowing users to switch between games effortlessly. 🏆🎯 Let the fun begin! 🚀🎮

## 🏰 Features 🎨🔥  
- 🔦 **Glassmorphism UI** – A sleek, modern, and visually appealing design.  
- 💻 **Fully responsive** – Adapts perfectly to mobile, tablet, and desktop screens. 📱💻  
- 🎮 **Game Selection Menu** – Pick any game with a single click! 🖱️🎯  
- 🌟 **10 thrilling games** covering logic, memory, speed, and fun challenges. 🧠🎉

## 🔎 Games Overview 🎲🧩  
Each game is crafted to **test your skills, sharpen your mind, and provide endless fun**! 🚀🎮 Here’s a quick look at what you can play:  

### 1️⃣ 🧙 Math Quiz 🔢✏️  
- **Objective:** Solve math problems correctly to earn points.  
- **Skills:** Arithmetic, logical thinking, and quick calculations.  

### 2️⃣ 📏 Guess the Number 🔢🤔  
- **Objective:** Find the hidden number in limited attempts.  
- **Skills:** Logical reasoning and probability estimation.  

### 3️⃣ ✊✋✌ Rock Paper Scissors 🎮🤖  
- **Objective:** Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors and play against AI.  
- **Skills:** Decision-making and strategy.  

### 4️⃣ 🔹🔺 Shape Matching 🟩🟠  
- **Objective:** Drag and match shapes to their outlines.  
- **Skills:** Pattern recognition and spatial intelligence.  

### 5️⃣ ❌⭕ Tic-Tac-Toe 🎭💡  
- **Objective:** Place three marks in a row before your opponent.  
- **Skills:** Strategic planning and critical thinking.  

### 6️⃣ 🃏 Memory Card Game 🧠🔍  
- **Objective:** Flip cards and match identical pairs.  
- **Skills:** Memory, concentration, and cognitive ability.  

### 7️⃣ 🏁 Maze Solver 🌀🚀  
- **Objective:** Navigate the maze and reach the finish line.  
- **Skills:** Problem-solving and spatial awareness.  

### 8️⃣ 💚 Word Scramble 🔠🧠  
- **Objective:** Rearrange scrambled letters to form words.  
- **Skills:** Vocabulary and spelling skills.  

### 9️⃣ ⌨ Typing Speed Test 🏎️💨  
- **Objective:** Type the given text as fast and accurately as possible.  
- **Skills:** Typing speed, accuracy, and reflexes.  

### 🔟 🎈 Balloon Pop Math 🎯🎈  
- **Objective:** Pop the balloons carrying the correct answer.  
- **Skills:** Quick math calculations and reflex actions.
